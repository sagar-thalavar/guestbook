# Guestbook – Requirements Discovery Log

This document records the discussions, decisions, questions, and finalized answers used to define the Guestbook project. It serves as the source of truth for project planning and future development.

---

# Project Concept

## Initial Idea

The project originated from the idea of allowing visitors to take a selfie directly within the application and display it publicly as a collage.

However, after further discussion, the concept evolved into a private and moderated Guestbook experience focused on personal memories rather than a public wall.

The intention is to:

* Demonstrate technical capabilities such as authentication, cloud storage, moderation workflows, notifications, and privacy controls.
* Create a meaningful archive of visitor memories.
* Build a portfolio-worthy project with real-world use cases.

# Visitor Experience

## Question

What should visitors see first?

### Original Draft

Leave a memory of your visit today with an expression of what you feel.

### Finalized Version

Capture a moment from your visit.

Leave a photo and a short message that reflects how you feel today, creating a personal record of your visit.

## Question

Should a selfie be mandatory or optional?

### Final Answer

Optional.

---

## Question

What information can visitors submit?

### Final Answer

* Original Name
* One-line Message
* Optional Selfie
* Optional Mood

---

## Question

Can users edit submissions?

### Final Answer

Yes.

However:

* Pending Submission → Editable
* Approved Submission → Locked
* Rejected Submission → Re-upload Required

# Data Collection

## Question

What information should be stored?

### Final Answer

Only essential information:

* Name
* Email
* Date
* Time
* Mood
* Message
* Submission Status
* Uploaded Image

Avoid unnecessary personal data.

---

## Question

Should visitors see visit history?

### Final Answer

Yes.

Visitors should be able to see their Guestbook history.

---

## Question

Should every website visit create an entry?

### Final Answer

No.

Only Guestbook submissions create entries.

Browsing the website alone is not considered a Guestbook visit.

---

# Moderation

## Question

What moderation workflow should be used?

### Final Answer

Submission

↓

Pending Review

↓

Admin Review

↓

Approved or Rejected

---

## User Notice

### Finalized Text

Thank you for your submission.

For privacy and safety reasons, all submissions are reviewed before they become visible in your Guestbook history. You will receive an email notification once your submission has been approved or if any changes are required.

---

## Question

What happens when a submission is rejected?

### Final Answer

The user receives:

* A rejection email
* The reason for rejection
* A link to re-upload

Replacement uploads are preferred over creating entirely new entries.

---

## Question

How many re-upload attempts are allowed?

### Final Answer

Maximum 3 attempts.

---

# Final Vision Statement

Guestbook is a privacy-focused digital visitor journal that allows authenticated visitors to record moments from their visit through photos, moods, and short messages.

The project combines authentication, moderation workflows, cloud storage, notification systems, audit logging, and user privacy controls while maintaining a simple, personal, and secure user experience.
