# Phase 3 – Create Guestbook Entry

## Goal

Allow users to create personal memories.

## Entry Message

Capture a moment from your visit.

Leave a photo and a short message that reflects how you feel today, creating a personal record of your visit.

## Entry Fields

### Required

* Original Name

### Optional

* Selfie
* Mood
* One-line Message

### Message Limit

* 200 characters

### Mood

Custom mood supported.

Examples:

* Happy
* Inspired
* Curious
* Excited
* Reflective

## Completion Criteria

Users can submit entries successfully.

# Phase 4 – Submission Rules

## Goal

Protect storage resources and prevent abuse.

## Limits

Per User:

* 1 submission per day
* 3 submissions per week
* 10 submissions per month
* 50 approved entries lifetime

## Limit Message

Display a friendly notice when limits are reached.

## Completion Criteria

Rate limits are enforced.

# Phase 7 – Moderation Workflow

## Goal

Review all submissions before publication.

## Workflow

Submission

↓

Pending Review

↓

Admin Review

↓

Approved or Rejected

## User Notice

Thank you for your submission.

For privacy and safety reasons, all submissions are reviewed before they become visible in your Guestbook history. You will receive an email notification once your submission has been approved or if any changes are required.

## Completion Criteria

Moderation process operational.

# Phase 15 – Portfolio Integration

## Portfolio Description

Guestbook is a privacy-focused digital visitor journal that allows authenticated visitors to record moments from their visit through photos, moods, and short messages.

The project combines authentication, moderation workflows, cloud storage, notification systems, audit logging, and user privacy controls while maintaining a simple and personal user experience.

## Completion Criteria

Project featured on portfolio.
