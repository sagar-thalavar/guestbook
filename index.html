Privacy & Archive Ethos

A secure digital journal crafted to respect visitor data rights.

Guestbook is a visitor-focused digital journal. Your entries, selfies, and moods are kept secure, moderated for safety, and stored as an archive of your connection with us. In alignment with modern data-rights philosophy, you hold full control to choose if your approved entry is shared publicly or kept private, all photos are served over secure signed URLs, and visitors hold full deletion rights over their entries.
