Privacy & Archive Ethos

A secure digital journal crafted to respect visitor data rights.

Guestbook is a private, visitor-focused digital journal. Your entries, selfies, and moods are kept secure, moderated for safety, and stored exclusively as an archive of your connection with us. In alignment with modern data-rights philosophy, there is no public community wall, all photos are served over secure signed URLs, and visitors hold full deletion control over their entries.
